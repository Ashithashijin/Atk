OUR JOURNEY OF SUCCESS

Since its inception in 2004 and the long journey, ATK Enterprises has maintained the highest standards of business values, integrity, understanding, excellence, unity, responsibility and service within the trading circle. Dedication to these ideals, an unwavering commitment to perform to the highest operational standards and a bold vision for the future is the foundation of ATK Enterprises' philosophy. The company is continuously rising on the growth chart by setting very high quality standards. Our strict emphasis on quality is combined with the latest technology to offer our customers a competitive edge. We focus on quality and aim to achieve total customer satisfaction, both in the products we offer and in the service that follows.

ABOUT ATK ENTERPRISES

OUR STRENGTH

ATK Enterprises have grown to the level where we manage a staff circle of over 150 people and 3 depots in Kerala. Our depots are located at Thalassery, Aluva and Ayikkarappadi and we supply goods to all districts in Kerala. We cater to the needs of 750 dealers all over Kerala from Kasargod to Trivandrum, incorporating with several companies and brands since 2004. Our prized banking partners are South Indian Bank, State State bank of India, and HDFC.
